The theme comes with a sleek and modern design and offers a powerful platform for tech enthusiasts, reviewers, and industry experts to share their knowledge and insights with their audience.

== Description ==

The Personal Coach Lite WordPress Theme is a streamlined, responsive, and user-friendly solution designed specifically for personal trainers, fitness coaches, wellness mentors, health consultants, strength and conditioning specialists, and online fitness professionals aiming to build a powerful online presence. Perfect for offering personalised fitness plans, weight loss coaching, post-injury rehabilitation, high-intensity interval training (HIIT), sports-specific conditioning, flexibility improvement, and nutrition-focused programs, this theme provides an ideal platform to promote one-on-one coaching, virtual fitness classes, fitness bootcamps, and tailored workout sessions. It features a modern, minimal design with customizable sections for showcasing client success stories, testimonials, training certifications, fitness blogs, coaching packages, and transformation galleries. Its mobile-friendly and retina-ready layout ensures smooth performance on all devices, making it easy for clients to connect, schedule sessions, or follow fitness tips on the go. Additional features support integration of meal plans, progress tracking tools, customizable workout calendars, and email newsletter sign-ups. Whether you're a holistic lifestyle coach, a certified personal trainer, or a fitness influencer building a brand, the SEO-optimized and fast-loading Personal Coach Lite WordPress Theme offers everything you need to market your services, engage with clients, and grow your coaching business online.

== Installation ==

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance -> Themes and click the 'Add New' button.
2. Type in Personal Coach Lite in the search form and press the 'Enter' key on your keyboard.
3. Click on the 'Activate' button to use your new theme right away.
4. Navigate to Appearance > Customize in your admin panel and customize to taste.
